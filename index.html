What is a Social Enterprise?

Social enterprises include nonprofits which run income-producing businesses and for-profits which prioritize positive social and environmental impact. (Rockpa, 2017)

Social entrepreneurship has rapidly emerged globally in recent years. While implementing entrepreneurial activities that pursue both social value and commercial value, take into account social and corporate nature, combine the realization of social value and corporate operation, social entrepreneurship actively carries out innovation in the fields of children care, employment of the disabled, and community development.

Social entrepreneurship has achieved social goals in a peaceful manner which were achieved through violence in the past. It has played an active role in solving social problems and will have a more profound impact on the development of human society.

“Social entrepreneurs are society’s change agents, creators of innovations that disrupt the status quo and transform our world for the better.”

- The Skoll Foundation

About Us

The Funding Network (TFN) is a not-for-profit organization to help social entrepreneurs get crowdfunded. It funds and supports social entrepreneurs of different types and development stages. It connects social entrepreneurs with social welfare organizations, social enterprises, foundations, business and professional organizations with relevant knowledge, resources and networks, and help to fund social change programs to promote sustainable social benefits.

Our Roles

-Ensure that all donations are donated to social entrepreneurs without deduction of any administrative expenses.
-Develop a strict and transparent funding and monitoring mechanism to ensure that the donations are allocated to the most needed social entrepreneurs.
-Develop a strict and transparent mechanism to review applications of social entrepreneurs.

(Morillo, 2018)
